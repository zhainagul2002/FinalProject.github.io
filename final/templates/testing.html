Testing

The Daily Health Check is part of a comprehensive strategy to limit the spread of COVID-19 on campus.

Diagnostic Testing

Diagnostic testing involves testing those who are showing symptoms of COVID-19 or who have been exposed to someone who has tested positive for COVID-19. Diagnostic Testing is done at Bonetsky's Labaratory by appointment only.

Surveillance Testing

Surveillance testing involves testing a random selection of people from across campus to identify asymptomatic and presymptomatic individuals and help avert potential outbreaks before they occur. Surveillance Testing is done at Bonetsky's Labaratory. When selected, you will receive a text or email with details.

Testing Elsewhere

Students

Students are strongly encouraged to test at the Bonetsky's Labaratory. If students have tested positive for COVID-19 at an off Bonetsky's Labaratory, it is critical they upload their external test results to help the University identify high-risk or close contacts, provide timely notifications, and offer guidance on quarantining or self-isolation to prevent further transmission in our campus community.

It is easy for students (both undergraduate and graduate) to share their external test results with us:

• Take a photo of your test result and sent an email to info@iaau.edu.kg. Instructions are as follows:

◦ Log into your AIU email.

◦ Click on the Document Upload Link.

◦ Write “COVID Test Results” on the Subject line.

◦ Select “Browse” and navigate to the test result image you would like to upload.

◦ Click “Send” after you have attached the document.

Faculty and Staff

Faculty and staff are strongly encouraged to inform the AIU Health Center if they test positive for COVID-19 regardless of where they have the test performed. This information will help the University’s contact tracers with the contact tracing process for any campus activity.

We want to make it as easy as possible for faculty and staff getting tested elsewhere to be able to share their test results with us:

• Take a photo of your test result and email it to the AIU info@iaau.edu.kg
